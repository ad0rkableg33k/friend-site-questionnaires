Selling & Payments

Heads up: Stripe, Square, and standard PayPal all prohibit glass pipes/bongs/bubblers as high-risk products — that's likely why eBay has worked so far, since marketplaces absorb that risk themselves. Options: (1) stick with eBay/Etsy as the payment backend and use the new site as a catalog that links out to purchase, (2) apply for a high-risk merchant account (e.g. PaymentCloud, Durango Merchant Services, Host Merchant Services) to get real on-site checkout — more paperwork and higher fees, but full control. Let me know which direction sounds right, or if you want to talk it through.
